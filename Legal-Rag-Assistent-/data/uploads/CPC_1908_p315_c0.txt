11. In Order XX of the Code, for Rule 1, the following Rule shall be substituted, namely:––
“(1) The Commercial Court, Commercial Division, or Commercial Appellate Division, as
the case may be, shall, within ninety days of the conclusion of arguments, pronounce judgment
and copies thereof shall be issued to all the parties to the dispute through electronic mail or
otherwise.”.
